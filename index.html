About the Foundation

Founded by actor Keanu Reeves, The Reeves Foundation is a private humanitarian initiative focused on supporting displaced children and families affected by war. Our mission is to restore dignity, safety, and opportunity to those forced from their homes by conflict.

Our Work

We work quietly and efficiently to deliver aid, build infrastructure, and provide emotional and logistical support to families in crisis. Our foundation is currently in its private launch phase with trusted internal donors and a clear, purposeful structure.
